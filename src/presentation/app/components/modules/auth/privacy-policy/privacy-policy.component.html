Information Collection

The application collects certain information when you download and use it. This may include:

  • Device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Pages visited within the application
  • Date, time, and duration of usage
  • Mobile operating system information

Additionally, the app may collect personal information necessary for core HR functionalities, such as:

Email ID: Used for secure user authentication and login

Camera Access:Used to capture real-time photos for attendance marking

Live Photos:Captured only during attendance check-in/out and used strictly for internal verification

Location Access (Optional):Requested at the time of marking attendance to verify physical presence at the workplace or designated site.

  • Granting access is optional
  • Users can deny location access and still mark attendance via live photo
  • If accessed, location data is not stored permanently or tracked in the background

Grievance Information Collection

The app also enables employees to raise HR-related grievances, which may include uploading documents or communicating via in-app chat. The types of grievances supported include:

  • Provident Fund (PF)
  • Salary-related concerns
  • ESIC queries
  • Joining
  • PSH

Any files or messages submitted for grievances are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized HR personnel.

GDPR Privacy

Under the GDPR, users have several key rights regarding their personal data. They have the right to be informed about how their data is collected, processed, and used. Users can request access to their data, ask for corrections if the data is inaccurate, and request deletion of their data when it is no longer needed or processed unlawfully ("Right to Erasure"). Users may also restrict the processing of their data under specific circumstances and have the right to data portability, allowing them to receive their data in a structured format and transfer it to another controller. Additionally, they can object to the processing of their data, particularly for direct marketing purposes.
